What is Headless Development?

Headless commerce is a modern eCommerce solution that separates the frontend from the backend. The decoupled design makes it easier for developers to build and personalize the customer experience without disrupting the backend systems.

Why Headless Commerce?

Headless commerce known for its unmatched flexibility, speed and scalability. It has been greatly helping modern businesses to create seamless, omnichannel experiences and adapt quickly to changing market demands.

Frequent Ask Questions?

What is Headless Commerce and how does it work?

Headless Commerce separates the frontend from the backend (eCommerce engine). It allows greater flexibility and faster performance rates.

What are the benefits of using Headless Commerce?

It enables complete design freedom, quicker and easier page loading, multi channel functionality and improved customer experiences.

Which platforms support Headless Commerce architecture?

Platforms like Shopify Plus, BigCommerce, Magento, and Contentful support headless implementations via APIs.

Is Headless Commerce suitable for startups?

Headless is the ideal and best option for expanding or enterprise businesses. Startups can initially find classic platforms more economical.

How does Headless Commerce improve site performance?

By using modern frontend frameworks (like React or Vue), headless setups can reduce load times and improve interactivity.

What frontend technologies can be used with Headless Commerce?

With the aid of contemporary frontend frameworks (such as React or Vue), headless configurations can lower load times and enhance interactivity.

Does Headless Commerce support omnichannel selling?

You may use React, Vue.js, Angular, Next.js, or custom frameworks to develop the frontend of a headless store.

Is Headless Commerce SEO-friendly?

Yes, if used correctly and implemented properly with server-side rendering (SSR) or static generation (SSG), headless stores are SEO-optimized.

How do I know if Headless Commerce is right for my business?

If your business requires adding some advanced customization, increased presence and speed and having capabilities to development, Headless could be perfect choice.
