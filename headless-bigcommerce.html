What Is Headless BigCommerce?

Headless BigCommerce is an advanced ecommerce configuration in which the front-end (what visitors see) is isolated from the back-end (where you enter products, orders, etc.). Its flexible design enables you to utilize contemporary technologies such as React.js, Next.js, or Vue.js to develop lightning-fast, highly customizable storefronts. Unlike conventional ecommerce sites where style and functionality are closely interdependent, headless commerce provides you with unlimited creative liberty, quicker loading speeds, smooth integrations, and an omnichannel ready-for-the-future solution.

Frequent Ask Questions?

What is Headless BigCommerce?

Headless BigCommerce is a configuration in which the front-end (what customers see) is decoupled from the back-end (commerce engine) that enables you to employ your own technologies such as React, Next.js, or Gatsby for the front-end.

What are the advantages of Headless BigCommerce?

It provides unparalleled flexibility, accelerated performance, enhanced SEO, better user experience, and seamless third-party integrations which is perfect for businesses that demand complete control over their storefronts.

Is Headless BigCommerce suitable for my business?

If your business requires custom functionality, enhanced user experience, or accelerated page speeds, Headless is the ideal option.

How is Headless BigCommerce different from BigCommerce?

Classic BigCommerce employs its native themes and templates, whereas headless enables you to create custom front-ends using your own design tools and frameworks ultimately giving you a great deal of flexibility and control.

Will Headless impact my SEO or performance?

Yes but positively as if it is built properly then it can significantly enhance SEO, performance, and Core Web Vitals.

Do I still get access to BigCommerce apps and features with a headless setup?

Absolutely! You still have access to BigCommerce's powerful features such as product management, checkout, and payment gateways. These are all accessed via APIs to your own custom front-end.

How secure is a headless BigCommerce site?

Very secure. BigCommerce takes care of PCI compliance and security on the backend, and your front-end can be constructed using current security best practices.
